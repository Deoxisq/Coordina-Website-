About Coordina

Coordina was born from the frustration of planning group trips with scattered information across multiple apps, emails, and group chats. We believe travel planning should be exciting, not overwhelming. Our mission is to simplify the way people organize and share their travel experiences, making every journey more memorable and stress-free.

Our Story

The Problem

Planning trips with friends and family often becomes chaotic. Information gets scattered across different platforms, important details are missed, and coordination becomes a nightmare instead of excitement.

Our Solution

Coordina brings everything together in one place. Create beautiful itineraries, invite your travel companions, and collaborate in real-time. Everyone stays in the loop, and nothing gets forgotten.

Our Vision

We envision a world where travel planning brings people together rather than stressing them out. Where the journey begins with the first shared idea, and every step of planning becomes part of the adventure.
