About Coordina

Coordina was born from the frustration of planning group trips with scattered information across multiple apps, emails, and group chats. We believe travel planning should be exciting, not overwhelming. Our mission is to simplify the way people organize and share their travel experiences, making every journey more memorable and stress-free.

Our Story

The Problem

Planning trips with friends and family often becomes chaotic. Information gets scattered across different platforms, important details are missed, and coordination becomes a nightmare instead of excitement.

Our Solution

Coordina brings everything together in one place. Create beautiful itineraries, invite your travel companions, and collaborate in real-time. Everyone stays in the loop, and nothing gets forgotten.

Our Vision

We envision a world where travel planning brings people together rather than stressing them out. Where the journey begins with the first shared idea, and every step of planning becomes part of the adventure.

Last Updated: September 30, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to CoordinaApp ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using CoordinaApp, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you use our app, we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password
  • Profile Information: Name, profile picture (optional)
  • Trip Planning Data:
    • Packing lists and checklist items
    • Itinerary details and calendar events
    • Budget information including expenses and planned spending by category
    • Trip names, destinations, and dates
  • Group Collaboration Data:
    • Friend invitations and contact information
    • Group member names and roles
    • Shared trip planning content

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our app:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: IP address, app crashes, system activity

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide trip planning, packing lists, itinerary management, budget tracking, and group collaboration features
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account
  • Synchronization: To sync your data across devices using Firebase
  • Collaboration: To facilitate group trip planning and sharing with invited friends
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality
  • Communication: To send important updates about your account or the app
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain data security

Data Storage and Security

Firebase Infrastructure

We use Google Firebase as our backend database service. Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers and is subject to Google's security measures and infrastructure.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
